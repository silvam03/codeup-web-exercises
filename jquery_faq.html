4. How many acres does the National Park Service protect?
Over 84 million!
5. What National Park is the oldest?
Yellowstone National Park!
6. Which two states have 8 National parks?

8. Which National Park has the longest known cave system in the world?

Yosemite National Park

  • Yosemite's granite rock formations glow like fire at sunset!
  • It's landscape supports more than 400 species!
  • One of the few places you can see a rainbow at night!
  • Famous for its giant sequoia trees!

Grand Canyon National Park

  • The Grand Canyon is bigger than the state of Rhode Island!
  • The most dangerous animal in the park is the rock squirrel!
  • There are over 1000 caves in the Grand Canyon!
  • 5.9 million people visit per year!

Denali National Park and Preserve

  • One of the best places in the U.S. to watch the Aurora Borealis!
  • Only one species of amphibian (Wood Frog) has adapted to life in the cold at Denali!
  • The Glaciers cover over 1 million acres of Denali!
  • Some of the park rangers are dogs!
